In Level 1, you will manipulate the main player to move across the landscape game scene. There are 2 enemies in this level, which are fly and snail. Try to avoid touching the ply, as you will lose one life for that. Though hitting snail will also lose one life, you can jump onto it to destroy enemy and get points. They are also coins in this level, which by collecting them you will get points. Moreover, they are few lottery box up top, which you will randomly redeem the prize in it. The prize includes diamond, which you will get points, heart that will earn one more life. However, you will lose life for getting the upset face icon. That is pretty much it. Let’s rock!

In level 2, you will manipulate main player to move across the screen. Unlike level 1, you can only preform left move and right move. You can use the button with up arrow to fire bullet. They are two enemies in this level, which are the fly and the angry box. You will lose life for hitting fly, but earn points by launching bullet on it. You can also fire the moving angry box for earning points. You can fire the coin box to collect coins for points. At the end, you will be able to fire the dropping boxes for points. Try to avoid the bombs, which you will lose life for that. Ok, let get it started.
